Installation and plan effect

And sometimes it happens that we have chosen some plan, some set of actions or attitudes that seem to work for someone else,
someone grows, rises up, but it does not work for us.

So, to begin with, this attitude or plan needs to be analyzed, evaluated,
so to speak, to notice where something is wrong, and then replaced or adapted to something that may be closer to you.

We all have different experiences, different pictures of the world,
and different words and actions that respond or work for us.

Recipe for getting rid of procrastination Rest → think about whether I really need it and what for (find a reason if necessary) →
check if you have a plan, how you follow it and what in it may not work → ask your inner critic to be quieter.
